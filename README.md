# Calgary Business License Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes business license data from the City of Calgary
to identify trends in economic activity, business distribution,
and sector dominance.

## Dataset
Source: City of Calgary Open Data Portal

The dataset includes:
- Business name
- License type
- Status
- Issue and expiry dates
- Community

## Tools Used
- PostgreSQL
- SQL
- Data cleaning
- Data analysis

## Key Findings
- Over 80% of business licenses are active or renewed
- Retail, food, and service sectors are the most common
- Beltline, Foothills, and Downtown are major business hubs
- Business license activity increased strongly after 2021

## Data Limitations
- Some records have missing issue dates
- 2026 data is incomplete
- Some businesses have multiple license types
